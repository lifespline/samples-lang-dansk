Farve/Colour
============

* ``mørk``: dark
* ``lys``: light
* ``lyse``: light
    * ``lyse ris``
* ``brun``: brown
* ``brune``: brown
* ``brunt``: brown
* ``blåt``: light
    * ``et blå dyr``
* ``blå``: blue
    * ``det blå dyr``
* ``gult``: yellow
    * ``mit æg er gult``: my egg is yellow
* ``gul``: yellow
    * ``en gul appelsin``: a yellow orange
* ``rød``: red
    * ``de er rød``: they are red
* ``røde``: light
* ``hvide``: white
    * ``hendes sko er hvide``: her shoe is white
